👤 About

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the ARISE Lab, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), advised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu. I received my B.Eng. (First Class Honours, AI: Systems and Technologies) from CUHK in 2023.

My research focuses on LLM coding agents and deep research agents, with emphasis on multi-agent collaboration, context engineering, scaffolding, and post-training. I am particularly passionate about leveraging multimodal LLMs to automate UI/web code generation and intelligent game development.

I am currently interning at Tencent Game AI Center, building LLM agent-driven game development systems. Previously I interned at Tencent AI Lab, where I developed a self-improving deep research agent.
